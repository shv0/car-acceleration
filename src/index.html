Vehicle acceleration modeling calculator

v. 1.0.1

This calculator allows you to simulate the acceleration of a car using its basic parameters.
To get started, enter the parameters of the car in the form below, or try out the calculator with standard parameter values. Then click Simulate.
You can evaluate the traction-speed properties of the car from the charts or the values in the table.
